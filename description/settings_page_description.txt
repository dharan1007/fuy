## ðŸ“± Mobile Settings Screen
**File Reference:** `mobile/app/settings.tsx`

The Settings screen allows users to configure their account, appearance, and privacy preferences.

### 1. Account Section
*   **Edit Profile:** Navigates to the multi-step profile editor.
*   **Notifications:** Navigates to notification preferences.
*   **Safety & Privacy:** Accesses safety tools (Blocklists, etc.).
*   **Verify Identity:** Opens the Face Verification Modal for identity confirmation.

### 2. Appearance Section
*   **Dark Mode:** Switch to enable system-wide dark theme.
*   **Light Mode:** Switch to enable light theme.
*   **Eye Care Mode:** Switch to enable a warmer, sepia-toned theme for reduced eye strain.

### 3. Support Section
*   **Help & Support:** Displays a toast with the support email (`contact@fuy.com`).
*   **Log Out:**
    *   Triggers a "Log Out" confirmation modal ("Are you sure you want to log out of the universe?").
    *   On confirm, signs the user out and redirects to Login.

### 4. UI Design
*   **Glassmorphism:** Uses `BlurView` containers for section grouping.
*   **Theme Aware:** Background gradients adapt to the selected mode (Light/Dark/Eye Care).
*   **Icons:** Color-coded icons (Blue for Profile, Rose for Notifications, etc.) for quick visual recognition.
