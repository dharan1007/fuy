## SECTION 1 - WHAT DO WE DO WITH YOUR INFORMATION?

When you create an account and use our messaging application, we collect personal information necessary for providing our services. This includes:

- **Account Information**: Your name, email address, profile picture, and user bio
- **Communication Data**: Messages you send and receive, conversation history, and attachments
- **User Relationships**: Your followers, following list, and friendship connections
- **Presence Data**: Your online/offline status, typing indicators, and last seen timestamps
- **Technical Information**: Your device information, internet protocol (IP) address, browser type, and operating system details to help us maintain service quality and security
- **Usage Analytics**: Information about how you use our application to improve user experience

We collect this information to enable real-time messaging, manage your social connections, provide search functionality, and deliver an optimized user experience across our platform.
